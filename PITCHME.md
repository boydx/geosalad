## Terminology
* **Dataset** is editable
* **Tileset** is symbolized
* Tilesets can contain many layers


---
### Formats we can use (open source)
* **Vector**: Shapefile, GeoJSON (edited) at EPSG: 4326
* **Raster**: GeoTIFF, EPSG: 3857
