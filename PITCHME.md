#HSLIDE
##NDVI Analysis
* Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) 
* Color infrared photography (CIR) with 4-BandNAIP imagery
* Use Raster Calculator to calculate (B4 - B1)/(B1 + B4)
* High density of vegetation is good in the city!
